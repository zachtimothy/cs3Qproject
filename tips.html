  Tips and tricks


In Minesweeper, there are numerous tricks that can help you in finishing the game.



There are numerous patterns that can be seen with the use of
numbered tiles, some basic while are others advanced:

Since we know there's a '1' placed in the corner of a closed tile, we can conclude that the closed tile is a mine.

Similar to the last picture, we can say that these three tiles are mines due to the surrounding numbered tiles.



A common strategy for starting this game is to randomly click until you open an empty tile, which would clear a large number of tiles for you.
(beware though as this version of minesweeper does not protect your first move, meaning that you can trigger a mine at the start of the game)
